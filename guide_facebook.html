Guide to Live Stream on Facebook with Qveedo

Welcome to Qveedo, Let’s get started with a step-by-step guide to unlock your streaming potential.

Step 1: Go to Facebook on your computer and click on Live Video

Navigate to Facebook and click on Live Video to begin the setup.

Step 2: Click on Go Live button

Click on the Go Live button to proceed with the live streaming setup.

Step 3: Select Streaming Software

Choose Streaming Software to use the Stream Key with Qveedo.

Step 4: Copy the Stream Key

Copy the Stream Key from Facebook to use in Qveedo.

Step 5: Paste the Stream Key into our app

Paste the copied Stream Key into the Qveedo app, then click on Next and start streaming.

Step 6: Go Live!

Once you see your device on your computer, click on Go Live and start streaming.
