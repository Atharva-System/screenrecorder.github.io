Account Deletion Process on Mobile Application

Dear Customer,

I hope this message finds you well.

We would like to inform you that the functionality to delete your account is already available on our mobile application's settings page. To initiate the account deletion process, please follow these steps within the mobile application:

1. Open the mobile application.
2. Navigate to the settings page.
3. Locate and select the "Delete Account" option.

By following these steps, you will be able to submit a request for the deletion of your account. If you encounter any difficulties or have further questions, please don't hesitate to reach out to our customer support team at contact@atharvasystem.com

Thank you for choosing our services, and we appreciate your understanding.
