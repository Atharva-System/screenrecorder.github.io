How to Stream Live on Rumble with Screen Recorder

Welcome to Screen Recorder, Let’s get started with a step-by-step guide to unlock your streaming potential.

Step 1: Go to Rumble and click (+) button and click on Go Live

Navigate to Rumble, click on the "+" button, and select "Go Live".

Step 2: Click on Create Live Stream and fill up your streaming details

Click "Create Live Stream" and fill out the necessary streaming details.

Step 3: You’re one step away! Click on Get Streamer Configuration and get stream details right away.

Click "Get Streamer Configuration" to access your stream details.

Step 4: Copy Stream URL and Stream Key

Copy the Stream URL and Stream Key provided by Rumble.

Step 5: Paste the Stream URL and Stream Key to our app and enjoy live streaming

Paste the copied Stream URL and Stream Key into the Screen Recorder app and enjoy live streaming.
