How to Stream Live on ReStream with Screen Recorder

Welcome to Screen Recorder, Let’s get started with a step-by-step guide to unlock your streaming potential.

Step 1: Go to ReStream and click Profile Icon at top right hand side, Tap Settings

Open ReStream and tap the Profile Icon at the top right, then tap "Settings".

Step 2: Tap on Streaming Setup and copy RTMP URL and Stream Key

Tap on "Streaming Setup" and copy the RTMP URL and Stream Key.

Step 3: Paste RTMP URL and Stream Key to our app and enjoy live streaming

Paste the copied RTMP URL and Stream Key into our app and you're ready to go live!
