How to Stream Live on ReStream

Welcome to Qveedo, Let’s get started with a step-by-step guide to unlock your streaming potential.

Step 1: Go to ReStream and Tap on New Stream

Open ReStream and tap on the New Stream button to start setting up your stream.

Step 2: Tap on Encoder | RTMP and get your streaming configuration

Tap on Encoder | RTMP to access your streaming configuration settings, including the RTMP URL and Stream Key.

Step 3: Copy and Paste RTMP URL and Stream Key

Copy the RTMP URL and Stream Key from the configuration settings.

Step 4: Paste RTMP URL and Stream Key to our app and enjoy live streaming

Paste the copied RTMP URL and Stream Key into our app, and you're all set to enjoy live streaming!
